Tribute to Dr RS Praveen kumar

Introduction

Repalle Shiva Praveen Kumar is a formar officer of Indian Police Service as the secretary of Telangana Social Welfare Residential Educational Institutions Society and Telangana Tribal Welfare Residential Educational Institutions Society.

Life Achievements

President's Police Medal for meritorious service

Telangana Excellence Award

Conclusion

During tenure as the secretary of TSWREIS and TTWREIS, these residential schools being run for students belonging to marginalised sections has scripted many success stories and concentrated on wholesome personality development of the students.He held the role about 10 years.
